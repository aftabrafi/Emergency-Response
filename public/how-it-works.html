How Emergency Help Works

Follow these simple steps to ensure quick and reliable emergency support.

Step 1: Register

Why register? Creating an account lets you securely store emergency contacts and access location-sharing features.

How to register? Click the **Sign Up** button, enter your details, and verify your phone number or email.

Step 2: Enable Location Tracking

Why enable tracking? Sharing your live location helps emergency responders reach you faster.

How to enable? Go to **Settings**, activate location services, and allow the app to track your position.
